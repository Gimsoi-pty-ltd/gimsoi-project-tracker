# PRD — PM Analytics Fast-Track

## 1. Goal
Surfacing actionable workload, pacing, and priority data in Sprint Reports.

## 2. Fast-Track Requirements
- **Workload**: Group story points/tasks by assignee. Render as "Team Workload" table.
- **Pacing**: Swap KPI cards to show `Task Progress (Hrs)` and `Delay Rate`.
- **Urgency Sort**: Order task list: `BLOCKED > REVIEW > IN_PROGRESS > TODO > DONE`.
